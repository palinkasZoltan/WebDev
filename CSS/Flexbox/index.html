  • An alien is a being from a planet other than Earth, often depicted as having extraterrestrial features and advanced technology.

  • A clown is a comic performer known for their distinctive makeup, colorful costumes, and amusing antics, often seen at circuses and children's parties.

  • A toy mouse is a small plaything shaped like a mouse, usually made of fabric, plastic, or rubber, for entertainment or pet interaction.

  • A ghost is a supernatural entity or spirit of a deceased person, often depicted as translucent, intangible, and haunting a specific location.
